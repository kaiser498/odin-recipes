Spaghetti Aglio e Olio (Garlic and Oil Spaghetti) Recipe


Description:

A simple yet flavorful Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. It's quick, easy, and perfect for a light meal.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) extra virgin olive oil
  • 4-5 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and chili flakes, cooking until garlic is golden (about 2-3 minutes).
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat. Add reserved pasta water as needed to create a light sauce.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and garnish with parsley. Serve with Parmesan if desired.

This recipe is quick, delicious, and perfect for any occasion!
