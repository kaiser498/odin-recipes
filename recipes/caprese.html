Caprese Salad Recipe


Description:

A fresh and vibrant Italian salad made with ripe tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil, drizzled with balsamic glaze and olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz (225g) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze (or balsamic vinegar)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating and overlapping them.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
