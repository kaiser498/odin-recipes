Lasagna Recipe


Home

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. This recipe will guide you through making a delicious, homemade lasagna from scratch.


Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 lb (450g) ground pork (or substitute with more beef)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz/170g) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz/425g) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) red wine (optional)
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 4 tbsp (60g) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (950ml) whole milk
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembling:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or boiled according to package instructions)
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps to Make Lasagna:

Step 1: Prepare the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent (about 3-4 minutes).
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the ground beef and pork, breaking it up with a spoon. Cook until browned and no longer pink.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, red wine (if using), basil, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer the sauce on low heat for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasoning if needed.

Step 2: Make the Béchamel Sauce

  1. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the flour and whisk continuously for 1-2 minutes to create a roux.
  3. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook the sauce until it thickens (about 5-7 minutes).
  5. Stir in the nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. (Skip this step if using oven-ready noodles.)

Step 4: Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  4. Spread a layer of béchamel sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce.
  5. Sprinkle a handful of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Repeat the layers (noodles, béchamel, meat sauce, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.

Step 5: Bake the Lasagna

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  3. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.

Step 6: Serve

  1. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
  2. Serve warm and enjoy your homemade lasagna!

This lasagna is rich, flavorful, and sure to impress. Pair it with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal. Buon appetito!s
